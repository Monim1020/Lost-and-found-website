How to Use the Site

  • To report a lost or found item, go to the Report Item page.
  • To view all items, visit the Items page.
  • Use the Search & Filter page for advanced search.
  • Check your Profile for your reports.
  • Admins can manage items and users in the Admin Dashboard.

FAQs

  • How do I contact someone about an item? Use the contact info on the item details page.
  • How do I edit or delete my report? Go to your profile page.
  • How do I get notifications? Check the notifications page for updates.
